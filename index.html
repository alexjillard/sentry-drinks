Ingredients
  • 0.5 oz Honey
  • 0.75 oz Lemon Juice
  • 3 drops of Orange & Grapefruit Bitters
  • 2 oz Amaro Montenegro
  • Soda & Prosecco
Instructions
  1. Combine Amaro, honey, lemon juice and bitters by stirring (the honey gets stuck) in a shaker.
  2. Add ice to the shaker to chill the drink and pour into a glass.
  3. Top with Soda and/or Prosecco. Approx 3-4 oz.
  4. Garnish with mint leaf.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 Peeled Peach
  • 3 Strawberries
  • 1/2 Lemon, juiced
  • 2 oz Cold Black or green tea
  • 0.5 oz Cranberry Juice
  • 1 oz Vodka
  • Soda
Instructions
  1. Muddle the peach and strawberries in a pitcher
  2. Pour in the other ingredients (except the soda) into the pitcher and stir
  3. Strain into a glass with ice and top with soda
  4. Garnish with a peach/strawberry wedge
Crash-Free Session
  • Omit the Vodka

  • 1 oz Orange Liqueur
  • 1.5 oz Cranberry Juice
  • 1.5 oz Gin
Instructions
  1. Pour all ingredients into a shaker with ice
  2. Shake vigorously until the shaker feels ice cold
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass with ice.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel.
